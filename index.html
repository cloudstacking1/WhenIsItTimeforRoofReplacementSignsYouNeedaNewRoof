What Are Roof Shingles?

Roof shingles are overlapping elements that cover a roof. Traditionally made from asphalt, these shingles protect your home from water, wind, and other environmental factors.:

    Asphalt Shingles:

    The most common type, affordable, and versatile.

    Wood Shingles:

    Known for their natural appearance but can be higher maintenance.

    Metal Shingles:

    Durable, energy-efficient, and often chosen for their modern aesthetic.

    Slate Shingles:

    Very durable and long-lasting, though more expensive.

Roof Repair and Roof Leak Repair: Keeping Your Roof in Top Shape

Your roof is constantly exposed to the elements, and over time, wear and tear can cause damage. Common issues like cracked or missing shingles, water stains inside your home, or even visible leaks can be a sign that your roof needs repair.

One of the most common roof problems is roof leaks. Roof leaks can be caused by damaged shingles, cracked flashing, or even poor installation. If left unaddressed, roof leaks can lead to serious issues like mold growth, water damage, and structural problems.
